About Us

VitaLink is a comprehensive health monitoring web application designed to empower individuals in managing their personal health data efficiently and effectively. Our platform allows users to track and store results for four key health tests: BMI, blood sugar, heart rate, and urine analysis. With VitaLink, users can create, read, update, and delete their test results, as well as visualize their data through intuitive charts. Our mission is to provide a seamless and user-friendly experience that helps individuals stay on top of their health metrics, identify trends, and make informed decisions about their well-being. Join us on our journey to revolutionize personal health management with VitaLink.
